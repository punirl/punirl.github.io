About NIRL

The New Instrument Research Lab (NIRL) at Princeton University is headed by Jeff Snyder, the Director of Electronic Music and the Director of the Princeton Laptop Orchestra (PLOrk). The lab focuses on the creation of new instruments for electronic music performance, with emphasis on embedded synthesis and physical interfaces.
